### Usage

#### Counter

`javac Counter.java`

`java Counter`

#### Flips

`javac Flips.java`

`java Flips 10000`

#### FlipsMax

`javac FlipsMax.java`

`java FlipsMax 10000`

#### Rolls

`javac Rolls.java`

`java Rolls 100000`

#### Cat

`javac Cat.java`

`java Cat In1.txt In2.txt Out.txt`

#### Date

`javac Date.java`

`java Date 7 27 1991`

`java Date 7/27/1991`

#### Accumulator

'javac Accumulator.java`

`java Accumulator 1000'

#### VisualAccumulator

'javac VisualAccumulator.java`

`java VisualAccumulator 2000'

#### StaticSETofInts

'javac StaticSETofInts.java`

`java StaticSETofInts tinyW.txt < tinyT.txt'
